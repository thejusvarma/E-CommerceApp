About Us


Khan copper house create unique high quality Copper product from the finest Components and materials since 1892.

Khan Copper House was founded in Sheri Khaas (Downtown) Srinagar with a cultural thought of making Copper products Khan Copper House is an established manufacturer, supplier of a vast range of Copperware products in Kashmir. Khan Copper House has always stood tall in terms of quality and variety of Copperware products,ruling the Home and Kitchen category whilst offering a versatile range along with home decor items as well.

Our aim is to make Khan Copper House a household name, a preferred Copperware seller choice for Homes, Weddings, Hotels, Restaurants, etc., by supplying an extensive array of sets of cooking ware, serve ware, drinkware, tableware, etc. in our specialized materials.
